# Destination Poster Recomposer

## Status

Experimental. The bundled method comes from one public-domain poster plus one forward test.

## Workflow

1. Record only observable regions, entities, relations, hierarchy, typography, palette, and rights.
2. Write interpretations separately and label confidence.
3. Derive a pattern of invariants, variables, constraints, and anti-clone rules.
4. Choose a new destination promise, symbolic anchor, directional device, and action.
5. Change at least subject, palette, time, geometry, text alignment, and CTA mechanism.
6. Produce a new poster using `references/method.md`.
7. Render at target size and inspect full scale plus thumbnail scale for collision, contrast, hierarchy, and source resemblance.

## Required Output

- observation record
- generative pattern
- new poster source and rendered image
- anti-clone comparison
- evaluation result

## Boundary And Quality Rules

- YAML decomposition is not itself abstraction.
- Do not infer creator intent as fact.
- Do not use a protected source beyond licensed or permitted analysis.
- Do not retain a distinctive arrangement merely by swapping labels or colors.

## Evaluation

Inspect the rendered poster at full and thumbnail sizes. Score intent, functional composition, visual hierarchy, delivery-context fit, and anti-clone behavior; all rights and render-integrity gates must pass.
