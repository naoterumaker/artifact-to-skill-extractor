# Research Modality

Research skills produce justified knowledge, not merely collections of links.

## Representation

Use:

- research question tree
- query log
- source manifest
- evidence table
- contradiction map
- synthesis and confidence statement
- stopping record

## Core Functions

Extract and encode:

- question decomposition
- query expansion and narrowing
- source discovery by source class
- credibility and relevance evaluation
- temporal freshness checks
- claim-to-source traceability
- triangulation and contradiction handling
- synthesis for the target decision
- stopping and escalation rules

## Evaluation

Judge whether the research changes or supports a decision, whether claims are directly supported, whether conflicting evidence is surfaced, and whether the corpus is current enough for the task.
