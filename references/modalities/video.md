# Video Modality

Represent video with a time-based map. Do not reduce it to a transcript: pacing, shot transitions, sound, and visual promise are part of the artifact.

## Required Views

- timeline with time codes
- shot or scene map
- spoken claim and on-screen evidence relation
- music, silence, effects, and energy curve
- open loops and resolutions
- title/thumbnail or entry-promise alignment when distributed on a platform
- retention-risk moments and information density

## Abstraction

Extract temporal relationships such as:

```text
promise -> proof preview -> context -> escalating evidence -> resolution
```

Variables include subject, scene, duration, speaker, proof type, visual treatment, and pacing. Constraints preserve promise delivery and comprehension.

## Evaluation

Evaluate the first impression, first 30 seconds, scene-to-scene progression, audio/visual coherence, promise fulfillment, and intended platform objective. Validate real retention only from analytics after publication.
