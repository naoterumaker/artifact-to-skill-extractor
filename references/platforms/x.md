# X Platform Adapter

Use this adapter only after selecting a text/image/video modality and a concrete objective. Do not treat "X style" as a universal production method.

## Intake

Establish:

- format: short post, thread, or long-form article
- primary objective: impressions, reading depth, bookmarks, replies, follows, profile visits, or outbound clicks
- account topic, current audience, and relationship to readers
- baseline performance and successful/failed examples when available
- publishing constraints, factual risk, and call to action

## Research Axes

Before building an X content skill, research current evidence for:

- platform format and policy constraints
- audience demand and competing discourse
- opening relevance and reading continuation
- information or emotional payoff
- source and proof expectations
- mobile readability and visual attachment behavior
- objective-specific action design
- observable examples and counterexamples

Prefer current official guidance and actual account analytics. Use public posts, Reddit discussions, and practitioner reports as evidence with explicit confidence, not as universal algorithm facts.

## Pre-Publication Evaluation

Evaluate:

- opening promise and relevance
- move sequence and payoff
- unsupported claims
- readability at mobile width
- platform/format fit
- objective alignment
- originality and source attribution

## Post-Publication Evaluation

When analytics are available, record the selected KPI and contextual factors. Do not attribute performance to copy alone; account, timing, topic demand, and distribution conditions may contribute.
