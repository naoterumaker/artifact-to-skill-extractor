# Worked Example: X Article Creative System

## Test Question

Can one permitted X Article seed a scoped creative system that produces a materially different article without falsely recovering the source creator's philosophy or claiming live platform performance?

## Evidence And Ceiling

The source is a public Japanese X Article about source-led article production. `assets/examples/x-article/source-manifest.yaml` records its URL, creator, publication date, volatile public metrics, rights limits, and unverified claims. The repository does not redistribute the full body.

One finished article can support observed moves, source-specific expression, functional hypotheses, and candidate language/narrative grammar. It cannot establish longitudinal creator doctrine, causal performance, or a universal X formula.

## Native Representation

Argumentative text is represented as `assets/examples/x-article/move-map.md`. Markdown preserves sequence, rhetoric, evidence status, and interpretive uncertainty. YAML is used only for stable manifests and the older generative-pattern handoff; it is not required for the article body.

The source's topic, wording, title formula, named people, metrics, and examples remain source-specific. The candidate relation is:

```text
specific active pain
  -> challenge one causal assumption
  -> provide an evidence-scoped replacement mechanism
  -> organize distinct reasoning units
  -> resolve constraints or objection
  -> preserve provenance
  -> offer one proportionate action
```

## Compiled Creative System

The v0.3 intermediate representation is under `assets/examples/x-article/creative-system/`.

| Layer | Compiled result | Evidence status |
|---|---|---|
| Profile | `creative` | capability decision |
| Recovered creator doctrine | explicitly unavailable | insufficient evidence |
| Project doctrine | evidence integrity, attribution, reader autonomy, no performance guarantees | user-confirmed plus evaluation-derived |
| Grammar | pain-to-mechanism article relation with conditional body shapes | candidate |
| Decision unit | choose the strongest claim whose certainty does not exceed evidence | experimental |
| Toolchain | no external tool required for drafting; publication and analytics unsupported | explicit boundary |
| Execution graph | inspect source, select claim frame, draft, evaluate, bounded repair, human publication gate | complete structural contract |

The decision unit is executable because it names context, required evidence, selection conditions, action, invariants, verifier, fallback, provenance, confidence, and agency. A label such as `build trust` would not be sufficient.

## Generated Skill And New Concrete

The candidate skill is `assets/examples/x-article/source-grounded-x-article/SKILL.md`. The unseen input is this repository's evaluation-driven skill design rather than another version of the source topic. The output changes topic, evidence, mechanism, examples, title, voice details, and CTA while retaining only the candidate functional relation.

## Evaluator Loop

The rubric was frozen at `assets/examples/x-article/evaluation-rubric.json`.

| Round | Score | Decision | Root issue |
|---|---:|---|---|
| 1 | 75.20 | revise | unsupported three-times outcome claim and over-broad repair rule |
| 2 | 87.90 | pass | no blocking issue; live platform performance remains unknown |

Round 1 produced two different repairs:

1. The permanent method gained an evidence ladder and a ban on invented quantitative uplift.
2. The one-off article replaced a generic repair claim with lowest-responsible-layer routing.

The passing output is `assets/examples/x-article/final-round-2.md`. Its score is a pre-publication proxy, not evidence of impressions, dwell time, engagement, or conversion.

## What This Establishes

- Text requires a text-native representation, not image-style YAML.
- A single article can seed grammar and a decision hypothesis but not creator doctrine.
- Explicit project doctrine can govern a new system without being mislabeled as recovered philosophy.
- The functional relation can survive while protected expression and source-specific details change.
- A creative system can be executable without pretending it has a toolchain or live analytics capability.

The system remains experimental until additional article cases, a held-out doctrine prediction, independent review, and real publication evidence exist.
